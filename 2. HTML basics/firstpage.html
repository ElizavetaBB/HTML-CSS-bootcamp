English as a second language

Students who learn English as a second language are often limited in what and how they write. This leads them to use Chat GPT for each assignment and suppresses their creativity and critical thinking.

Benefits for practice grammar

As for grammar, Chat GPT is a great source to learn topics and get additional practice. Thought process behind the project or essay is more important than a few mistakes that a student made.
